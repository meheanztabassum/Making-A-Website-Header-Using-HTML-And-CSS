Maldives

With its stunning islands, mesmerizing beaches, and turquoise waters, Maldives is famous for its exciting water activities.Maldives is a low-lying island country in the north-central Indian Ocean. Its closest neighbours are India, about 600 kilometres north-east, and Sri Lanka, about 645 kilometres north-east. It is made up of around 1,200 small coral islands and sandbanks.
